What is Mystify?

“ The art of simplicity is a puzzle of complexity ”

Have you ever been to an escape room? You know how expensive it can get for an hour of fun. But what if you can have the fun of uncovering clues and demystifying puzzles for free over a period of 2 days. IEEE NITK presents the 3rd edition of Mystify!. The first edition of this unique, enthralling and mind-boggling puzzle competition to be held offline, we introduce a new format of puzzles as well. The only escape you'll be doing here is from your monotonous lives.

It's open to all branches of 1st & 2nd year students of NITK.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

This event is open to all branches of 1st and 2nd year students of NITK.

Is there a registration fee?

There is no registration fee.

What are the prerequisites?

No, there are no prerequisites.

Is it like CTF?

No, it is not like CTF.

How many members in a team?

A maximum of four members are allowed per team.

Do I have to be an IEEE member or need to have at least one IEEE member in my team to participate?

No, you do not have to be an IEEE member nor need at least one IEEE member in your team to participate. It is open to anyone and everyone in 1st and 2nd years.

How can we prepare for the event?

Just have an open-minded approach, think outside the box and be ready, to have fun!

What is the criteria for selection into Round 2?

Top 50% of the teams will move on to Round 2.

Do I get a participation certificate if I clear Round 1?

Clearing Round 1 alone doesn't guarantee a participation certificate. You have to solve at least 2 puzzles in Round 2 to get a participation certificate.

Do I need to have anything on me when coming for the event?

One laptop per team is a must - Windows, MacOS, or Linux. Having more than 1 laptop per team is advised but left up to the team's discretion. Bring some basic stationery like notepads, pens, etc.

Can we participate in Round 2 without coming to the venue?

Yes, you do not have to be at the venue to take part in Round 2. You can compete from wherever you are. But those who are at the venue will get Onesta coupons.
